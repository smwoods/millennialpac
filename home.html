Millennial PAC

Providing political capital for young Americans

Internships are unregulated. Most are illegal.

Young Americans can’t afford to donate to campaigns and don’t vote... Why would Congress listen to us?

Since the Supreme Court’s decision in Citizens United v. Federal Elections Commission (2010) prohibited restriction on independent expenditure groups fundraising and spending, Super PACs have dominated the political climate. Millennial PAC provides a voice for millennials, by millennials. Young voters, young non-voters, and those too young to vote all need a voice in the world of independent expenditures. We run comprehensive political programs to represent the age demographic without representation in Congress.

Employment

Most unpaid internships are illegal. They must be regulated up to federal standards.

Education

Student loans are now more of a financial burden for Americans than credit card debt. We need change.

Safety

Gun violence kills more young people than car accidents. With almost one mass shooting per day in 2015, we need to make our country safer. Just like the 83% of gun owners that want enhanced background checks, Millennial PAC supports the second amendment and safety.
