Young Americans can’t afford to donate to campaigns and don’t vote... Why would Congress listen to us?

Since the Supreme Court’s decision in Citizens United v. Federal Elections Commission (2010) prohibited restriction on independent expenditure groups fundraising and spending, Super PACs have dominated the political climate. Millennial PAC provides a voice for millennials, by millennials. Young voters, young non-voters, and those too young to vote all need a voice in the world of independent expenditures. We run comprehensive political programs to represent the age demographic without representation in Congress.

Outside Spending by Cycle, Excluding Party Committees

Employment

Most unpaid internships do not comply with federal standards. They must be regulated according to the threshold outlined in the Fair Labor Standards Act so we do not perpetuate normalized free labor. Find out more.

Education

Student loans are now more of a financial burden for Americans than credit card debt. We need lower interest rates, more affordable tuition, and innovative scholarship programs so young Americans can start a life without insurmountable debt.

Safety

Millennial PAC supports the second amendment and common sense. Gun violence has surpassed car accidents as a leading cause of death for young Americans. Congress needs to change the law that prevents researching gun violence.
